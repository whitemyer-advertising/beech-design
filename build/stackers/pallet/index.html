Pallet Stackers

Easy-to-operate solution for order picking and placing goods

Beech Pallet stackers, or lift trucks, can perform a variety of tasks with your pallet rack system. Use them to place goods into low- and medium-level racking or order picking. And they are ideal for tight warehouse environments.

Every one of our platform stackers features a unique combination of high-quality engineering and construction, built-in safety features, outstanding warranty, and competitive pricing.
